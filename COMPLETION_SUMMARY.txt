EVALUATION CRITERIA
================================================================================

LOGICAL COHERENCE (LC) [1-10]
- Evaluates technique ordering against cyber kill chain
- 10: Perfect logical flow
- 7-9: Minor ordering issues
- 4-6: Several techniques out of order
- 1-3: Significant logical flaws

OPERATIONAL REALISM (OR) [1-10]
- Assesses real-world feasibility
- 10: Highly plausible, aligns with known APT behaviors
- 7-9: Realistic with minor implausible elements
- 4-6: Mix of realistic and unrealistic techniques
- 1-3: Unrealistic or implausible sequence

SAME OBJECTIVE (SO) [Yes/No]
- Yes: Candidate shares same primary objective
- No: Candidate has different primary objective
- Objectives: espionage, financial-gain, disruption, etc.
